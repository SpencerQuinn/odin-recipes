Peanut Butter and Jelly

Description

A true classic. Can't beat a PB & J!

Ingredients

  • 2 Slices of wheat bread
  • 2 Tbsp Peanut Butter (Crunchy only)
  • 2 Tbsp Jam (Not jelly. Jelly bad. Misnomer eh?)

Steps

Step One

Take out bread. Spread peanut butter on one slice. Jam on the other

Step Two

Press bread together, spreads facing eachother, ensuring that the bread humps match up.

Step Three

Eat it in front of TV
