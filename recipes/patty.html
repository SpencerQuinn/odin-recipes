Patty's Chicken Salad

Description

A lovely medley of peppers, celery, carrots, asparagus, and soybeans intermixed with diced chicken and a soy sauce vinegrette.

Ingredients

  • 6 Chicken Breasts
  • 1 Bunch asparagus
  • 2 carrots
  • 2 Bell peppers
  • 3 Stalks of celery
  • 1 Cup Shelled Edamame
  • 1/4 Cup Olive oil
  • 1/4 Cup Sesame oil
  • 1 Tbsp Balsamic Vinegar
  • 1 Tbsp Soy Sauce
  • 2 Cloves Garlic

Steps

Step One

Grill chicken until thoroughly cooked through, then dice.

Step Two

Steam asparagus and edemame until tender. Dice all vegetables and place in large bowl.

Step Three

Mix oils with soy sauce and vinegar. Add garlic and stir.

Step Four

Toss chicken, vegetables, and sauce together in big bowl. Serve cold.
